Creating

Creating something merely to be able to say that you did is, to me, the most enthralling and fulfilling challenge you can take on. Turning intangible thoughts into something real, tackling self-imposed roadblocks, and using them to grow delivers unrivaled satisfaction.

For a long time, I held back. Fear of failure and not living up to my own expectations stopped me. Eventually, I realized the only thing needed to bring ideas to life is the desire to see them made.
